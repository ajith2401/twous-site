TwoUs

FAQ, Trust & Privacy

No chat. No feed. No pressure. Just one question a day.

The idea

What is TwoUs?

A simple way for a couple to feel closer. Every day you both get the same question, you each answer on your own, and then you see each other's answers together.

What is the point of it?

To help you understand each other a little better, one question at a time. The same question goes to both of you, so you can see how you each think about the same thing, sometimes alike, sometimes very differently.

Why does my partner's answer stay hidden until I answer?

Because a relationship grows when both of you show up, not just one. You both answer honestly in private, then discover each other's answers in the same moment. No one goes first, no one feels put on the spot, and you get to be surprised by each other.

What is it not?

It is not a chat app, not social media, not therapy. There is no feed, no likes, no follower counts.

Why only one question a day?

Because one real question creates one real conversation. More would just become another thing to scroll past.

Why can't I chat in it?

You already have WhatsApp for that. TwoUs is for the quieter things daily messaging never makes room for.

How it works

How do we get started?

Sign up, verify the code sent to your email, then connect with your partner's invite code (tap their invite link, or type the code on the sign-up screen).

Can my partner see my answer right away?

No. Both of you have to answer before either answer is shown.

What if my partner has not answered yet?

Your answer stays private until they do. You will see "Your answer is in", and you will get a nudge the moment they answer.

What is the reflection part?

After you both answer, you each share one thing: what surprised you about your partner's answer. That small step is where most couples say the real conversation begins.

What is Cherish?

A place to send a small, specific thank-you, up to once a day. They add up into a shared collection of good moments.

Where do our past answers go?

Into your Timeline, kept as a memory you can both look back on.

Can I answer with a photo or voice note?

Yes. Text, a photo, or a short voice note. Whatever feels right that day.

How much time does it take?

Usually just a few minutes a day.

What kind of questions will we get?

They start light and warm, like how you met. Over time they can go a little deeper.

Privacy and trust

Can you read our answers?

No. Your answers, reflections and thank-yous are for you and your partner only. To understand whether the app is working, we look only at usage, like whether you both answered. We do not read what you wrote.

Do you sell our data?

No. We do not sell your data, and there are no ads.

Do you use our answers to train AI?

No. Your answers are not used to train AI.

Is it encrypted?

Yes - end to end. Your answers, reflections, gratitude, and photos are encrypted on your phone with a key only you and your partner hold. We store only scrambled text and cannot read them. It turns on automatically once you have both opened the app - there is no switch to flip - and you can verify your private connection with the safety code in the Us tab. Full policy: privacy policy.

If things change

What if we stop using it?

Nothing is lost. Your memories wait for you in the Timeline.

What if we remove each other?

The space is archived. No new questions, but your shared memories stay viewable.

What if I delete my account?

Your personal info is removed. Shared memories may stay in anonymised form so your partner's timeline still makes sense.

Help

I did not get the email code.

Check spam, then tap "Resend code".

Today's question is slow to load the first time.

The first open after a while can take a few seconds to wake the server. It retries on its own. If it stays blank, pull down to refresh.
