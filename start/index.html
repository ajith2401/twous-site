Aham 🌙

Before you start, read this once. Together, if you can.

Aham is a simple way for a couple to feel closer.

No chat. No feed. No pressure. Just one question a day.

How it works

Every day you both get the same question. You each answer on your own. You cannot see your partner's answer until you have answered too.

That waiting is on purpose. A relationship grows when both of you show up, not just one. When you both answer honestly, you get to see how you each think about the same thing. Some days you will match. Some days you will surprise each other. That is the part that keeps you curious, and keeps you close.

A few things to know

  • Your answers are private. Only you and your partner can read them.
  • There are no right answers. The best answer is not the longest one. It is the honest one.
  • Short is fine. Slow is fine. Missing a day is fine too. There is no streak to break.
  • You always answer in private, so you never have to worry about saying it the "right" way.

Open the app, read today's question, and tell each other something true.
The rest takes care of itself.
